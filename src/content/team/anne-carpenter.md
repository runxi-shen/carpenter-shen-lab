Anne Carpenter is a Professor at Purdue University and Principal Investigator of the Carpenter-Shen Lab. She leads multiple major consortia (JUMP, OASIS, VISTA) and the Cells to Cures Engine, orchestrating collaborative research across academic institutions, pharmaceutical companies, and government agencies. Her lab has created open-source software (CellProfiler) and assays (Cell Painting) that have become foundational tools in the field of image-based profiling. Her work has contributed to discoveries now in clinical trials and has been cited tens of thousands of times.
