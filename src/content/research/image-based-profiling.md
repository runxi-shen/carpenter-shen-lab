---
title: "Image-Based Profiling"
description: "Extracting rich, quantitative morphological fingerprints from microscopy images of cells to reveal the effects of chemical and genetic perturbations at scale."
icon: "🔬"
order: 1
---

Image-based profiling is a methodology for extracting rich, quantitative information from microscopy images of cells. By measuring hundreds of morphological features from each cell, we create high-dimensional profiles that capture the phenotypic state of cells under different conditions.

These profiles enable us to identify connections between genes, chemicals, and diseases, accelerating drug discovery at unprecedented scale.
